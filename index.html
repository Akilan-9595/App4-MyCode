The following questions will be used to calculate the percentage of your approximate Type 1 Muscle Fibers vs Type 2 Muscle Fibers.

Type 1 Muscle Fibers are commonly called slow twitch muscle fibers used for endurance. Type 2 muscle fibers, also called fast twitch fibers, are used for speed and explosiveness. The ratio of these muscle fibers is determined both by genetics and training

You will be asked a series of questions of what your strengths in athletic activies are to reveal at the end your approxiamate muscle fiber composition.
